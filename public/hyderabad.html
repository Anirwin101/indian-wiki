History of Hyderabad

Hyderabad was founded in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah. It served as the capital of the Qutb Shahi dynasty and later became an important center under the Nizams of Hyderabad. The city has a rich historical legacy and is home to several architectural marvels.

Geography and Modern Development

Hyderabad is located on the Deccan Plateau and is known for its unique blend of old-world charm and modern infrastructure. The city is a major IT hub, often referred to as "Cyberabad," and is home to several multinational companies and startups.

Cultural and Culinary Heritage

Hyderabad is a melting pot of cultures, with a vibrant arts scene, traditional crafts, and a rich culinary heritage. The city celebrates festivals like Eid, Diwali, and Bonalu with great enthusiasm. Its cuisine, especially the biryani and haleem, is world-famous.
