History of Kochi

Lucknow has a fascinating history rooted in the Mughal and Nawabi era. It became a prominent city under the rule of the Nawabs of Awadh, who were great patrons of art and culture. The city played a significant role in the Revolt of 1857 against British rule. Today, Lucknow preserves its regal charm while evolving into a modern metropolis.
